Clockwork is a mod built on the framework of Valkyrien Skies 2 and Create, providing new opportunities for exploration, and new ways to discover your own path to adventure in your world. From new mechanics to new biomes, new creatures and new machines- when magical whimsy and technological ingenuity collide, Clockwork stands in their wake.

Presently, Clockwork is in an Open Beta state. Things will change, and some things may be unstable or unfinished- here be dragons!

Thank you for all of your support and your patience for all of these years. I hope you enjoy the ride! There's always more to come, and more adventure to find- this isn't the end... it's just the beginning.

# For Developers

Clockwork builds are hosted on VS2's maven (https://maven.valkyrienskies.org)

To include Clockwork in your mod, you can use the following gradle repository-

```groovy
repositories {
   //...
   maven {
      name = "Valkyrien Skies"
      url = "https://maven.valkyrienskies.org"
   }
   //...
}
```
